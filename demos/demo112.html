Using video as a Picture entity source, and as a pattern

Extensions: images, animation, wheel, filters

Notes:

  • Videos will not autoplay in mobile or tablet devices - some form of user interaction is required to trigger video play
  • The video-as-pattern (in the wheel) displays differently in IE compared to other browsers
  • Video won't display in Safari(Windows) browser

Additional notes:

  • Scrawl assumes that video files are hosted on the same server as the web page and does nothing to help prevent cross-origin restrictions
  • Youtube - it is against the YouTube terms of service to use anything other than the YouTube player to play content originating from the YouTube website.
  • Third party video player libraries are not (currently) supported by Scrawl. Libraries such as Video For Everybody, Video.js, JWPlayer and MediaElement.jsinclude fallbacks to play Flash videos in legacy environments. Scrawl will only use <video> elements as a source for displaying video on the <canvas> element.
