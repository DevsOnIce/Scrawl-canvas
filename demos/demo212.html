Hover mouse over image to trigger animation

Why is the text animation smoother in Firefox and IE11 than in Chrome/Opera/Safari? Chrome computes font sizes as integers; Firefox/IE11 compute them as floats. Because this demo is tweening the font size in line with the stack scale, the knock-on effects of this difference adversely affects the animation in Chrome/Opera/Safari.
